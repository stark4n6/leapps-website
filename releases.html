What is LAVA?

LAVA lets investigators and analysts browse and explore output reports generated by the *LEAPP family of forensic tools.

Supported Tools

  • ALEAPP — Android artifacts
  • iLEAPP — iOS/iPadOS artifacts
  • RLEAPP — ISP Returns artifacts
  • VLEAPP — Vehicle artifacts
